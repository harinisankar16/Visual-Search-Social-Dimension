Hi! Welcome to the experiment

You will see a display of faces. 
Your task is to search for the target face that is trustworthy.
When you find the target, respond to the location of the marker next to the face. 

press the LEFT arrow key if the marker 
is on the left side of the face. 

press the RIGHT arrow key if the marker 
is on the right side of the face. 

Respond as quickly and as accurately as you can.

Press the SPACEBAR to begin.
